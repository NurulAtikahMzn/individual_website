Interesting Figures of Great Wall

How old is the Great Wall

The Great Wall is about 2,700 years old in 2019.

The Great Wall span across what region

15 regions in north China:

Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Inner Mongolia, Beijing, Tianjin, Hebei, Shandong, Henan, Shanxi, Shaanxi, Gansu, Ningxia, Qinghai, and Xinjiang.

How long is the Great Wall

With a total length of 21,196.18 km (13,170.70 miles), equal to half the length of the Equator, the Great Wall of China is the longest feat of human engineering.

Height and width of the Great Wall

The average height of the walls is 6 to 7 meters (20 to 23 feet), and the highest is 14 meters (46 feet).

The average width is 6.5 meters (21.3 feet).

The 'Great Wall of China'

China’s Great Wall is known all over the world. This wall was built in many parts by the rulers there to protect their kingdom, which was gradually added, which has now become a castle shape. It was constructed from the 7th century to the 16th century. This great artwork is so strong, and huge that it was called the Great Wall of China. The vastness of this wall of China has also been seen by scientists from space, this man-made artwork is also visible from there.

It is composed of soil, stone, brick, wood and other materials. This huge wall extends from Dandong to the east and extends to the west. China’s wall extends to approximately 6400 kilometers, and it is 35 feet high. This wall is like a fort; its width is so great that 10-15 people can walk comfortably in it.
