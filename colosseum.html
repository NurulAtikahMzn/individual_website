The Colosseum, Italy

"Largest amphitheatre built during Roman Empire."

Things to Know About The Colosseum

Colosseum is made of

Made from stone and concrete, this magnificent monument was built with the man power of tens of thousands of slaves.

How spacious is Colosseum

This brilliant building had 80 entrances and could seat approximately 50,000 spectators who would come to watch sporting events and games.

These events included gladiatorial combats, wild animal hunts and, believe it or not, ship naval battles!

What's inside Colosseum

Below the Colosseum were numerous rooms and underground passages. Here is where the animals and gladiators were kept, waiting to meet their fate in the arena above.

Is Colosseum hot during day

To protect the spectators from the blistering sun and heat of Ancient Rome, there was the velarium – an awning that could be pulled over the top of the seating area to provide shade.
